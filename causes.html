Root Causes of Human & Child Trafficking

Poverty and Lack of Opportunities

Families living in extreme poverty often become vulnerable to traffickers promising jobs, education, or better living conditions.

Conflict and Displacement

Wars and natural disasters displace families, especially children, making them easy targets for exploitation.

Lack of Education and Awareness

Uninformed individuals are more likely to fall victim to trafficking schemes due to lack of knowledge about the dangers and signs.

Weak Law Enforcement

Poor regulations, corruption, and weak legal structures allow traffickers to operate with minimal risk of punishment.
