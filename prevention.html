Prevention of Child Trafficking

Community Awareness & Education

Educating the community is key to preventing child trafficking. Schools, parents, and NGOs must inform children about potential dangers and safe practices.

Role of Law Enforcement

Police play a crucial role by monitoring trafficking hotspots, conducting raids, and rescuing victims. Specialized anti-trafficking units are trained to handle sensitive cases.

Early Warning Signs

  • Unexplained absences or sudden behavioral changes
  • New expensive items without explanation
  • Fearfulness, anxiety, or submissive behavior
  • Lack of identification documents
